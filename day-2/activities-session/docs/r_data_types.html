Project Description

The Mack Creek salamander & curthroat trout project is a long-term monitoring effort along Mack Creek in H.J. Andrews Experiemental Forest. Measurements began in 1987 and 1993 for the cutthroat trout and Pacific Giant Salamander, respectively, with the project continuing through to 2019. This study is one of the longest continuous studies for salmonoid populations and provides valuable insight into trends related to anthropogenic disturbances.
